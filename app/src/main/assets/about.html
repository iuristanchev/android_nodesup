Usage

Using the tool is pretty simple you just input the ips/dns names that you want to ping/rdns/get mx and click refresh/check.

Failed pings will have OFF on the toggle buttons. Succeeded pings will have ON. Results may vary depending on your ISP and carrier if you use it trough 3G network.

Also you have one field where you can test a service with port.

Have fun!
